SYMPTOMS


DRY COUGH


A tickling sensation in the throat can make you have a dry cough.


HIGH FEVER


A fever is a body temperature that's higher than is considered normal.


SORE THROAT


A sore throat is pain, scratchiness or irritation of the throat that often worsens when you swallow.


COLD


The common cold is a viral infection of your nose and throat (upper respiratory tract).


DIFFICULTY IN BREATHING


Shortness of breath, or dyspnea, is an uncomfortable condition that makes it difficult to get air into your lungs.
